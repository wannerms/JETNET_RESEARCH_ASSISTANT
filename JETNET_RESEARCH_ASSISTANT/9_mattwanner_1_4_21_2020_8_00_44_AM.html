CITATION II OVERVIEW
By far the most popular of the Citations and perhaps the most popular corporate jet ever built. The Citation II can carry eight passengers up to 2,000 nm at a max cruise speed of 374 knots. Its two Pratt & Whitney JT15D-4 engines provide 2,500 pounds of thrust apiece on takeoff. It climbs quickly at a little more than 3,000 feet per minute and has a maximum service ceiling of 43,000 feet. The cockpit boasts 340 degrees visibility and simple controls for vital systems like fuel and de-icing. The Citation II can be configured to be flown by a single pilot, reducing travel/operating costs and allowing for more flexibility in flight planning.
